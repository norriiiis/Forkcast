Sunday prep

~90 min, once
  1. 15m

    Mise en place

    Wash and chop the shared produce — onion, garlic, peppers, spinach — and store it grab-and-go.

  2. 30m

    Batch-cook proteins

    Cook the chicken thighs and sausage in batches; cool and store, ready to flavor per recipe.

  3. 30m

    Cook bases & sauces

    Cook the jasmine rice and penne; build the curry and tomato sauce bases.

  4. 15m

    Portion & store

    Portion everything into containers, labeled by night. Weeknights are now ~10-minute assembly.
